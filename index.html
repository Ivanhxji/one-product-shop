FAQ

Technical questions answered

If your car has factory wired CarPlay or Android Auto — yes, 99% compatibility. Quick test: plug your phone into the car's USB with a cable. If CarPlay/Android Auto appears on screen, AutoLink Pro will work. Exceptions: BMW (some iDrive versions), Tesla (no factory CarPlay), and Huawei EMUI phones (no wireless Android Auto support).

2.4GHz = same frequency as Bluetooth, other car devices, and even your phone's hotspot. Result: interference, audio drops, Maps lagging 2-3 seconds behind. 5GHz WiFi6 = dedicated high-speed band with less interference. Faster data transfer, stable in traffic, latency under 100ms. This is why premium adapters ($100+) all use 5GHz — we give you the same tech at $79.99.

Yes. AutoLink Pro supports both CarPlay (iOS 10+) and Android Auto (Android 11+, with 5.8GHz WiFi6). One adapter, both platforms. It connects to whichever phone pairs first. Note: if your car only has wired CarPlay but NOT wired Android Auto, only CarPlay will work wirelessly. The adapter converts what's already there — it doesn't add new features to your car.

First time: 30-60 seconds (Bluetooth pair + WiFi handshake). Every time after: 5-8 seconds from engine start. No button to press, no app to open. Start the car — CarPlay/AA appears on screen automatically.

Under 100ms on 5GHz WiFi6 — imperceptible to humans. Music, podcasts, and phone calls sound identical to wired. Maps navigation voice is perfectly synced. If you've used a 2.4GHz adapter before and had stuttering — that won't happen here.

OTA = Over-The-Air firmware updates. When Apple releases a new iOS version that breaks compatibility (happens often), we push an update to fix it. You don't have to do anything — the adapter updates itself. Most budget adapters can't do this — when iOS breaks them, you buy a new adapter. Ours stays working.

The compact design helps with heat dissipation. It's designed for automotive use — tested in hot conditions. The adapter sits behind the dash where airflow from AC helps. No reports of overheating from 1,173 verified buyers.
