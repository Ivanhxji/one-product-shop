2.4GHz = same frequency as Bluetooth, other car devices, and even your phone's hotspot. Result: interference, audio drops, Maps lagging 2-3 seconds behind. 5GHz WiFi6 = dedicated high-speed band with less interference. Faster data transfer, stable in traffic, latency under 100ms. This is why premium adapters ($100+) all use 5GHz — we give you the same tech at $59.99.
